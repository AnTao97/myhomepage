About Me


I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate in i-VisionGroup of the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jie Zhou, Prof. Jiwen Lu, and Prof. Yueqi Duan. Prior to joining Tsinghua, I got my B.Eng. from Southeast University.

My research focuses on 3D computer vision, mainly 3D scene understanding.
